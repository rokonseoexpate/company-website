eCommerce SEO FAQS

Q. What are eCommerce SEO services?
Ecommerce SEO services are a set of practices, techniques and strategies aimed at optimizing an eCommerce website to improve its visibility and ranking on search engines such as Google, thereby driving more organic traffic and increasing online sales and revenue. This is done by optimizing various elements of the website, such as the website structure, content, keywords, images, and meta descriptions, to make it more search engine friendly and appealing to potential customers.
Q. Why is search engine optimization (SEO) important?
If you own an eCommerce website, being on the first page of Google for your most important keywords isn’t an option – It’s mandatory.Think of it this way: A website is essentially an interactive multimedia advertisement for your business. Before that advertisement can be effective, you have to send it out. By implementing SEO strategies and tactics on your website, you make it easy for web surfers to find your site and see what your business has to offer. An outstanding website is great to have, but potential customers must be able to find it easily!
Q. Can you help improve my website speed and Google Core Web Vitals?
Absolutely! The speed of your website, along with general site usability, is becoming a major ranking factor. With the rollout of Google Core Web Vitals, Google shows your speed and usability scores via Google Search Console. Our SEO’s and developers will optimize your site to score at a high level, boosting organic rankings, conversion rate, and overall user experience.
Q. Why do I need an eCommerce SEO expert?
Your eCommerce website is much different than a typical website. You have a web of content from informational pages to products and categories. Optimizing each of these sections and connecting them together in a logical way (i.e., one that “pleases” search engine algorithms) is extremely important for the success of any eCommerce SEO campaign. With hundreds of eCommerce search marketing clients, we have the ability to look at what has worked for them and apply the same strategies to your website.
Q. How does OuterBox perform search engine optimization?
There are many ways we work to boost your organic rankings. First, we must research your market to develop an appropriate eCommerce SEO strategy. We work to find the best keywords for your business and use these keywords in the sections of your website most viewed by search engines. We also build the infrastructure of your website in a way that helps search engines easily detect and list all of your pages. We’ll also work with you to create web partners. Relevant websites that link back to your website bring your site’s credibility up.
Q. Can ecommerce SEO services improve my website’s ranking overnight?
No, ecommerce SEO services cannot improve your website’s ranking overnight. SEO is a gradual process that requires consistent effort and optimization over time. It may take several months to see significant results.There are a wide variety of factors that play into the amount of time it will take for your search engine rankings and traffic to improve. Those factors include the competitiveness of your industry, how long your site has existed, and where you currently rank. For example, if you are on page 5 we will work to move you to page 1, but through this process, you will go from page 5, to page 3 and eventually to page 1. As we make progress, traffic typically doesn’t increase until we hit the first page of the search results.
Q. How do you do eCommerce SEO for product pages?
When optimizing an eCommerce website, well-optimized product pages are critical to success. Depending on your industry, different tactics and eCommerce SEO strategies will apply. For example, many manufacturers or part dealers drive organic search traffic by ranking for SKU numbers and part numbers, while in other industries, part numbers may not be commonly searched. We will work to first identify which keywords we need to target for your product pages, then implement a strategy that matches. We also utilize dynamic optimization tactics that can make changes on all product pages at once, allowing us to ‘SEO’ hundreds or thousands of product pages simultaneously. The end goal is to drive customers from the product page to checkout as quickly as possible.
Q. How much do ecommerce SEO services cost?
The cost of ecommerce SEO services can vary depending on the services you need and the size of the website. Some companies offer packages starting at a few hundred dollars (usually small agencies or agencies outsourcing overseas), while experienced agencies typically charge between $2,500 – $20,000+ per month.
Q. What factors determine the success of an SEO campaign?
The success of an eCommerce SEO campaign depends on various factors, including the quality of the website to begin with, the age of the website, the competitiveness of the market, the size of the website, the brand authority, and the target audience. The more competitive the market, the more effort and resources required for optimization.
